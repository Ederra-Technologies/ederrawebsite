Why Us

We understand the AI marketplace and focus on setting new standards for servicing it.

Following our extensive research we understand the following points are the pivotal requirements for the industry.

  • Quality

    Label accuracy is the most crutial part while training the AI. We assure a 99% accuracy rate with our dedicated and trained QA professionals and proven procedures

  • Reliability

    99.9% availability in service on contracts guaranteed. We also have round the clock support to address any concernes you may have.

  • Productivity

    Our flexible workflow helps boost our output enabling and enhancing your teams productivity as well.

  • Scalability

    We have a pool of trained annotators who can be engaged to scale up on a moment’s notice based on your requirements.

  • Security

    We offer secure storage servers either locally or on cloud, per your preference. NDA’s will ensure privacy of your data.
